ABOUT

Ross is a multi-media producer specializing in visual asset creation, concept art, 2d animation, and web development. Originally from New Jersey, he currently lives in Philadelphia, PA USA. When he isn't nose deep in his computer, tirelessly working on his comic book franchise, he is likely suited up at the cockpit of his bass-station conjuring the spirit of the flying spaghetti monster. There is also the possibility that he is doing NEITHER, but in fact stuffing his face with sushi.
